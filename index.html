Meet Our Esteemed Speakers

Introducing our esteemed keynote speakers, pioneers in the field of artificial intelligence, set to share their valuable insights at our 'Future is Now' conference.

Prof. Andrew Lee

Prof. Andrew Lee is a renowned authority in the field of artificial intelligence, with particular focus on natural language processing. He's a professor at MIT and has led several projects that have had a profound impact on understanding how AI can interpret and generate human language. In his talk, he will share insights about the future of AI in education and how it can personalize learning.

Dr. Jane Smith

Dr. Jane Smith is a leading expert in machine learning and has worked on groundbreaking research at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biology. Currently, she is the director of the Artificial Intelligence Research Lab at Stanford University. She has published over 100 scientific papers and holds multiple patents in the AI field. Her keynote will explore the future of AI in healthcare and how it can transform patient care and outcomes.

Ms. Lara Hughes

Ms. Lara Hughes is a trailblazer in the field of AI ethics. As the head of AI Ethics at Google, she is responsible for ensuring the responsible and ethical use of AI in the company's products and services. Lara's presentation will focus on the challenges of ethical AI design and deployment, discussing important considerations like fairness, transparency, and accountability in AI systems.
